
\chapter*{Appendix A}
\label{sec:orga9f7ce7}
In this appendix we add the exact results of the simulations for each one of the benchmarks and each of the configurations we used.
Note that the non-mapped version of the benchmarks have the quantum gates decomposed in the operations allowed in the SC-17 chip.
Also, notice that not all the benchmarks have given results for the simulations with a decoherence time of \(10 \mu s\) due to the long simulation times.
